## Passion for the Blues: My Guitar Journey
Beyond the realms of academia and AI innovation, you’ll find me lost in the soulful rhythms of the blues. For me, the guitar isn’t just an instrument — it’s a bridge between logic and emotion, a medium to channel the raw, unfiltered energy of life. I’ve spent countless evenings locked in my room, fingers dancing across the fretboard as I chase the perfect bend or riff until my mom had to practically drag me out for dinner. To me, blues is more than music; it’s a language of storytelling, where every note carries the weight of joy, struggle, and resilience.<br>

My musical heroes are legends who’ve shaped the genre: B.B. King, Eric Clapton and John Mayer. Their influence resonates in my playing, whether I’m jamming to a classic 12-bar shuffle or experimenting with ambient textures.<br>

When I’m not coding or dissecting research papers, you’ll find me strumming a blues progression, letting the music wash over me — a reminder that even the most analytical minds need an outlet for creativity. After all, life isn’t just about hitting the right notes in code; it’s about finding harmony between the head and the heart.
